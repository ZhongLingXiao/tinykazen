**tinykazen** makes use of the following third-party libraries and data. 

* [embree](https://github.com/embree/embree)
* [ocio](https://github.com/AcademySoftwareFoundation/OpenColorIO)
* [oiio](https://github.com/OpenImageIO/oiio)
* [openexr](https://github.com/AcademySoftwareFoundation/openexr)
* [osl](https://github.com/AcademySoftwareFoundation/OpenShadingLanguage)
* [tbb](https://github.com/oneapi-src/oneTBB)
* [zlib](https://zlib.net/)
